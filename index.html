About Me

Aras Mustafa Hussein, a 24-year-old medical student in my 5th stage at Koya University, College of Medicine. Alongside my medical journey, I’ve actively led and participated in various public health projects under IFMSA Kurdistan-Koya,These experiences have deepened my belief that health is a human right and should be accessible to all. Beyond medicine, I’m also a passionate human rights activist. I advocate for equality, access to education, and the empowerment of marginalized voices in my community. I believe that true health goes beyond the absence of disease—it includes dignity, justice, and opportunity for all.

My Participations

November 14, 2023

World Diabetes Day Health Festival

As a local public health officer (LPO) of the IFMSA-Kurdistan, I managed a health festival for the world diabetes day, in this day we provided up to 450 free medical testes (RBS, BP, Visual acuity, measurement of BM and general health advices), in the campus of Koya University and we collected the data from the campaign to do a research on the vital signs and lifestyle of students and people in Koya city.

October 18, 2023

Mental Health Awareness Project

As LPO of IFMSA-Kurdistan, we did a project on mental health, as we know a man is a what he thinks about therefore mental health should be a priority for building better generation and society, we did a number of different activities including a seminar on mental health and some other activities like story telling and social interaction.

October 4, 2023

Selected as Local Public Health Officer

We proudly selected as a new Local Public Health Officer (LPO) for the term 2023 IFMSA-Kurdistan-Koya. Our dedication and commitment towards better public health made the members put trust in us and achieve great success hand in hand.

August 25-27, 2023

National Assembly of IFMSA-Kurdistan

During August 25-27, 2023, we participated in the national assembly of IFMSA-Kurdistan in Duhok. This important event inspired me to think globally and act locally. We were delighted to see projects and activities of other local boards of the organization.

2023

Conference of 2025 Vision of Hasar

As a volunteer in Hasar organization, I actively participated in the conference of 2025 vision of Hasar. We talked about our ideas and strategies to improve the quality of the project and suggested an eco-friendly business for the organization to be able to run its projects efficiently.
